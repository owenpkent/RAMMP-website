What is RAMMP?

A bold vision to transform independence for millions of people with disabilities and those who care for them.

The Challenge We Face

Today, millions of people are being held back by technology that hasn't kept pace with their potential.

Outdated Technology

Current powered wheelchairs and mobility devices are based on technology that hasn't fundamentally changed in decades. They force people to rely heavily on others, often restricting their world to just a portion of their own home.

Lives on Hold

Many people with disabilities must schedule their entire day around when a caregiver is available. Simple things most of us take for granted‚Äîgrabbing a snack, going to the store, or enjoying a meal‚Äîrequire planning and assistance.

The Caregiver Crisis

Caregiver availability is limited, often unreliable, and shrinking. Family members sacrifice careers and personal time. Professional caregivers are in short supply. As our population ages, this crisis will only deepen.

Barriers Everywhere

Curbs, uneven sidewalks, steep ramps, narrow doorways‚Äîthe physical world is full of obstacles. Current devices can't safely navigate these challenges, leading to tips, falls, and injuries that restrict where people can go.

Our Vision: A World of Independence

Imagine a world where having a disability doesn't mean giving up your freedom. Where you can go where you want, when you want. Where you can grab a snack from the fridge, go shopping with friends, or head to work‚Äîon your own terms.

RAMMP‚Äîthe Robotic Assistive Mobility and Manipulation Platform‚Äîis being built to make this vision real.

This isn't just an incremental improvement. It's a complete reimagining of what assistive technology can do. By combining advanced robotics, artificial intelligence, and smart mobility into one powerful platform, RAMMP will give people the ability to navigate their world and interact with it in ways that were never before possible.
